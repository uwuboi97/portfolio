    • This work is an amalgamation of two existing works. Artwork Description (not more than 300 words) collects artwork descriptions and translates them into intergers and maps them into a place in space. Layered with it is CONFESS THE PURPOSE OF PITY which presents collected words written by the artist, the texts change at intervals, and the background slowly consumes the overwritten texts exploring the form of unreaderly poetics. The works view language through either its pragmatic or poetic function, and explores this dichotomy through deconstruction
