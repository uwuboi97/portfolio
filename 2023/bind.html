    • New Media, Singapore Art Week 2023 The Upside Space
    • How to bind a book—Performance Lecture is a stylised video documenting the creation of Dorian, 2023, and presents research elements of traditional bookbinding and typography. The video is superimposed into a digital cathode-ray tube (CRT) television and finally projected onto an HD TV.

      This work is part of a three work series called Dorian, a commentary on our romanticism of the physical and antiquated, more information can be found at https://theupsidespace.com
