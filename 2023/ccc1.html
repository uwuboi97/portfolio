    • Installation
      Critical Craft Collective, Earl Lu Gallery
    • Artwork description (not more than 300 words) is an exploration of language as a social institution, and the underlying system of purely fictitious unity to express ideas. The work translates collected artwork descriptions from Draft #01 into unicode data, and maps the integers into vertices and finally into geometric forms.

      The work celebrates the various manifestations and parts of language and speech to finally form what we perceive as a concept. Thus, the nodes of visual programming are made lucid and part of the work, placed crudely beneath the final outcome; the system shall be part of the form, and the work, a machine.
