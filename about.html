    • Billie Sng (b. Singapore, 1997) is a multidisciplinary artist, writer, and designer whose research dissects the formal structures of language, poetics, and methods of writing. He has explored these topics through asemic writing (without meaning), self-language modelling, self-publishing, and new-media audio/video works.

      He graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts (First Class Honours) from LASALLE College of the Art (2022), and a Diploma in Communication Design from Temasek Polytechnic (2018).
