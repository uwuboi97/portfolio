    • artist

    • Billie Sng (b. 1997, Singapore) is an artist who graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts (First Class Honours) from LASALLE College of the Art, in partnership with Goldsmith, College of London (2022). His interests lie in language, text, and its emotions, and explores it through asemantic poetry, analysis of the formal structures of language, and through abstract methods of writing.
      He has exhibited at Gilman Barracks during Singapore Art Week 2023 under The Upside Space; Goethe Institut Singapore; Singapore Art Week 2024 and at the Earl Lu Gallery for Critical Craft Collective; Alliance Française Singapour, and was commissioned by the National Arts Council for the New Arts Plan 2023. He is also part of a Singapore-based art/tech collective, Mouse Click Click.
