    • Billie Sng is a Singaporean artist and designer who graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts (First Class Honours) from LASALLE College of the Art, in partnership with Goldsmith, College of London (2022), and a Diploma in Communication Design from Temasek Polytechnic (2018). His interests lie in writing, language, and poetics, and explores it through asemantic texts (without meaning) and abstract methods of writing. Throughout his interdisciplinary practice, he has explored AI language modelling and coding, typography design and publishing, new-media art and sound.
